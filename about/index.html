Ryan French,
A Developer and Designer from New Zealand.

bio

Ryan French is a Front-End Developer and Interactive Designer with 5 years experience in the industry. Originally interested in design, Ryan gained a bachelor of Design and Innovation in Digital Media Design. He combines both the skillset of a Designer and Developer to build beautiful and functional digital interfaces.

In the final year of his degree he gained an internship with Sharesight. This involved both designing and developing the marketing website. After Sharesight, Ryan moved on to work with web studio - Abletech. This involved working as a web consultant with several other companies developing and building web interfaces. After his time at Abletech, Ryan moved on to work with Xero, an online accounting software solution in the cloud. Ryan was in the marketing development team which involved building digital campaigns for xero.com.

Ryan has a passion for designing and building beautiful digital experiences that function as well as they look. This includes building websites that look great and function on a wide range of mobile devices. He strongly believes that form follows function.

Ryan is currently looking for a new oppurtunity.
